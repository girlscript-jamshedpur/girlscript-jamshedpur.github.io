About Resume & Profile Building

Introduction

It is imperative today to have a strong profile in career building. With this thought in mind, GirlScript Jamshedpur has come up with an event on Resume and Online Profile Building. It is an estimable benefaction by our team to the continuously changing career demands.

Event Report

The event was conducted for three days from the 10th to the 12th of July. It was designed as a power-packed hand on resume building session along with a highly inspirational guest session. The guest speaker was Ms. Diya Shlokam, who left no stones unturned in delivering her best to the highly spontaneous and enthusiastic aspiring minds.

Along with this, there was an exciting participation contest where top performers were featured on the GirlScript social media handles. Also, participation certificates were awarded to the attendees for their active involvement in the event. It was a pleasure for us to contribute something valuable to the field of profile building
